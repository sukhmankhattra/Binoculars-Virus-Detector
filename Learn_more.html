What makes Binoculars Security different from other antivirus solutions?

Binoculars Security combines multiple layers of protection including behavioral analysis, machine learning, and cloud-assisted scanning to detect both known and emerging threats. Unlike traditional antivirus that relies solely on signature detection, our solution adapts to new threats in real-time. We also prioritize system performance, with scans that are up to 3x faster than competitors while using fewer system resources.

How often does Binoculars Security update its threat database?

Our threat database receives updates every 15 minutes, 24/7, ensuring you're protected against the latest threats as soon as they emerge. Additionally, our machine learning models are continuously updated in the cloud, providing protection against zero-day threats even before specific signatures are available.

Yes! Our Pro plan covers up to 5 devices (Windows, Mac, Android, and iOS), while our Enterprise plan offers unlimited device protection.
